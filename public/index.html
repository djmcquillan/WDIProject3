About SPARK

SPARK is a "random act of video." The idea is simple. Login in to our site, and we will select a random TED talk to display to you. If this idea lights your fire, you can view local meetups in your area which correspond to the video category

Each time you enter the site, you will see a different talk on a random category. You may not always find a match, but the ideas will always be hot!

Features in development are to allow discussions online in real-time to strike-up conversations regardless of geography, so that ideas will spread like wildfire!
